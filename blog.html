Frequently Asked Questions

What people usually asked about us.

What is StepsStamp?
StepsStamp is a revolutionary platform where you earn crypto rewards for moving! Built on Binance Smart Chain (BSC), StepsStamp uses Proof of Movement (POM) every step you take counts as 1 POM to unlock rewards.
What is Proof of movement?
Proof of Movement (PoM) is an innovative concept that leverages blockchain technology to incentivize physical movement. It's a new consensus mechanism that rewards users for their physical movement. One Proof of movement is equal to 1 Step taken by the user.
Do I get any tokens for indoor workouts like treadmill running etc?
No. Indoor workouts are reflected in your step count, however they dont qualify as Normalized Steps required to mint the SSB token. Tokens are only rewarded for natural steps taken to go from point A to Point B.
How does the app track my steps?
Our app uses the phones built-in sensors, such as the accelerometer and gyroscope, to detect movements that match a walking pattern. This data is then processed by our proprietary algorithm, which translates as a Normalised step count.
Why does my step count increase when I shake the phone?
Phone sensors detect all motion, and shaking can sometimes mimic the pattern of walking steps. However, our algorithm eliminates artificial movements and displays only the normalised step count on the app.
How can I get the most accurate step count?
For the best accuracy, keep your phone in a stable position, like in a pocket or attached to your body. Avoid frequently moving it around or shaking it, as this can interfere with accurate step detection.
Can the app tell the difference between walking and other activities like cycling?
Yes, the app uses activity recognition APIs that can detect different activities, including walking, running, and cycling. These APIs help filter out non-walking activities so the step count remains accurate. SSBT(StepsStamp Blockchain Tokens) can only be mined via actual walking activity.
Does the app work when its running in the background?
Yes, our app can continue to count steps and track your activity even when its running in the background, provided youve granted the necessary permissions for background activity.
Why is there a discrepancy between my steps on this app and other fitness apps?
Each app may use slightly different algorithms and settings for step counting. Differences in sensor sensitivity, activity filtering, and distance thresholds can lead to minor variations in step counts across apps.
How often does the app update my step count?
The app updates your step count in real time as it detects new steps. However, for better battery life, step counts are sometimes batched and updated periodically.
Does the app use my location data to calculate steps?
While the app primarily uses motion sensors to calculate steps, location data may be used periodically to verify movement accuracy and improve step detection. This data is only used for accuracy purposes and is not shared or stored.
What permissions are required for step counting?
The app requires access to motion and fitness data for step counting, and location permissions are needed for enhanced accuracy in tracking movement. Background permissions may also be required for continuous tracking.
Will step counting drain my battery?
Step counting is optimized to minimize battery usage by using low-power motion sensors. However, continuous background activity and frequent location checks may use more battery, especially on longer tracking sessions.
How accurate is the step counting?
Our app aims to achieve high accuracy by using both motion sensors and activity recognition. Small differences may occur based on the phones position, the environment, and specific activities, but we strive to provide reliable step counts.
How do I report an issue with step counting accuracy?
If you notice inconsistencies, please reach out via our Support section in the app, and provide details about the issue. Were constantly working to improve accuracy, and your feedback is valuable.
What is a StepsStamp Blockchain (NFT)?
A StepsStamp Blockchain (NFT) is a unique digital asset that lets you mine SSBT by walking. Each Blockchain has 450 blocks, and activating a block requires 1,500 POM/day.
How does mining work with Blockchains?
Only Blockchain owners can mine SSBT. Mining rewards are decentralized and fixed:
  • Total Daily Mining Supply: 7,664 SSBT/day (distributed for 120 years).
  • Your Daily Reward: Depends on your Blockchain type:

Why 120 years?
The 120-year timeframe ensures long-term sustainability and rewards for loyal users.
How long does it take to complete a Blockchain?
At least 450 days (1 block/day). Consistency is key!
Can I activate multiple Blockchains at once?
No. Only one Blockchain can be active at a time.
What happens after I finish 300 and 450 blocks?
Youll receive 150 Blocks in Mini StepsStamp Blockchain(New NFT) after 300 Blocks completed and 75 Blocks in Micro StepsStamp Blockchain(New NFT) after 450 Blocks completed as rewards. Use them to keep earning or transfer them.

What are the differences between White, Black, Silver, Gold, and Green Blockchains?
Higher-tier Blockchains cost more but offer greater daily mining capacity of SSBT rewards and perks (e.g., StepsStamp Watches).
Can I sell my Blockchain (NFT) to someone else?
Yes! Inactive Blockchains or Mini or Micro Blockchains(NFTs) can be transferred or sold to others.
What happens if I lose access to my Blockchain (NFT)?
Your Blockchain is stored securely in your wallet. If you lose access, use your recovery phrase to restore it.
How do I purchase a Blockchain during Phases A-E?
Use BUSD to buy Blockchains. After Phase E, only SSBT will be accepted.
What if I miss Phases A-E?
After Phase E, youll need SSBT (purchased from exchanges) to buy Blockchains.
Are Phase bonuses time-limited?
Yes! Bonuses (welcome Phases Bonus, Investor Bonus, StepsStamp Watches) are exclusive to purchases made during their respective phases.
How do I earn referral rewards?
Share your referral link. When someone buys any Blockchain (NFT) using your link, you earn SSBT tokens equal to a percentage of their purchase in BUSD, based on your Blockchain tier:
  • White/Black Tier: 7%
  • Silver Tier: 8%
  • Gold Tier: 10%
  • Green Tier: 15%
Example:

If you own a Green Blockchain and your referral buys a White Blockchain (100 BUSD), you earn:
15% of 100 BUSD = 15 BUSD Converted to SSBT at the current price (e.g., Phase A: 0.05 BUSD/SSBT) 300 SSBT sent to your wallet!

Are there limits to referral rewards?
No! Refer as many friends as you want.
When do I receive referral rewards?
Immediately after your referral purchases a Blockchain.
Can I track my referral progress?
Yes! Check the Referrals tab in the app to see your earnings and pending rewards.
How is the Welcome Bonus in Phase A calculated?
The Welcome Bonus equals 20% of the Blockchain price in BUSD, converted to SSBT at the Phase A rate (1 SSBT = 0.05 BUSD).

Example: Green Blockchain costs 10,000 BUSD 20% = 2,000 BUSD 2,000 / 0.05 = 40,000 SSBT.

What are StepsStamp Watches, and how do they work?
StepsStamp Watches are exclusive gifts for Silver, Gold, or Green Blockchain holders. They give you free daily SSBT rewards without walking, but hurry theyre limited!
  • Silver Watch (1,000 units): 10 SSBT/day for 300 days.
  • Gold Watch (500 units): 50 SSBT/day for 450 days.
  • Green Watch (250 units): 100 SSBT/day for 730 days.

First come, first served! Claim yours before theyre gone.

When will I receive my StepsStamp Watch?
StepsStamp Watches will be delivered after Phase C concludes. Limited quantities are available first-come, first-served!
What is the Investor Bonus in Phase A?
A daily SSBT reward paid for 30 days after purchasing a Blockchain. For example:
Gold Blockchain: 167 SSBT/day 30 days = 5,010 SSBT.
What is the Total Withdraw Limit in Phase A?
The maximum 10% BUSD you can swap for SSBT per Blockchain tier. For example:
Green Blockchain: Swap up to 1,000 BUSD worth of SSBT.
What is the SSBT price in Phase A?
Phase A SSBT price: 1 SSBT = 0.05 BUSD.
How do I calculate my SSBT rewards in Phase A?
All rewards (Welcome Bonus, Watch, Investor) are paid in SSBT. Use the Phase A rate to estimate their BUSD value:
Example: 40,000 SSBT (Green Welcome Bonus) = 40,000 0.05 = 2,000 BUSD.
What happens after Phase E?
After Phase E:
Payment Method: Only SSBT tokens can be used to purchase Blockchains.
SSBT Price: Determined by the open market (exchanges).
What is DSSB?
DSSB is a decentralized bank where you become a shareholder>5,173 SSBT/day for 120 years.
How does staking work?
Daily Staking Pool: 5,173 SSBT (fixed supply).
Distribution: Proportional to your stake.
Payout Time: Daily at 00:00 GMT.
What are manual and auto staking?
Manual Staking:
     Example:

    You stake 40,000 SSBT in a pool of 1,000,000 SSBT.

    Your share: 4% Daily reward = 5,173 SSBT 4% = 206.92 SSBT.

    Your stake remains 40,000 SSBT unless you manually add more.


Auto Staking:
     Example:

    You stake 40,000 SSBT in a pool of 1,000,000 SSBT.

    Daily staking reward: 206.92 SSBT.

    Daily mining reward: 120 SSBT (if applicable).

    New Stake Next Day: 40,000 + 206.92 + 120 = 40,326.92 SSBT (rewards compound automatically).

How do I become a DSSB shareholder?
Stake SSBT tokens for 24 hours to start earning rewards.
Can I unstake my tokens anytime?
Yes! Unstaking takes 24 hours, and tokens return to your wallet.
What payment methods are accepted?
Only BUSD (Binance USD) is accepted for purchasing Blockchains.
How do I buy BUSD?
Purchase BUSD on crypto exchanges like Binance, then transfer it to your wallet.
Are there transaction fees?
A small BSC network fee applies for blockchain transactions (typically <$1).
Can I get a refund if Im unsatisfied?
Blockchain purchases are final due to their digital nature. Review details carefully before buying.
How long do payments take to process?
BUSD transactions are usually confirmed within 2-3 minutes on BSC.
What payment methods are accepted during Phases A-E?
During Phases A to E, you can purchase StepsStamp Blockchains (NFTs) only with BUSD. After Phase E ends, only SSBT tokens will be accepted for purchases.
What happens after Phase E?
Payment Method: Only SSBT tokens can be used to buy Blockchains.
SSBT in the Open Market: SSBT will be listed on exchanges, and its price will be determined by market demand and supply.
Are Watch rewards affected by my daily steps?
No! Watches reward SSBT automatically, even if you dont walk.

SSBT is the official reward token of StepsStamp. Earn SSBT by hitting POM(Proof of Movement) goals, then stake, withdraw, or spend it in the ecosystem!

Daily rewards are divided into two pools:
Pool A (1,5009,999 POM): Rewards shared proportionally based on your POM.
Pool B (10,000+ POM): Rewards shared equally among qualifying users.
